<objective>
Wire the Claude chat streaming into the voice loop and trigger the proactive menu overview.

Purpose: This replaces the Phase 2 placeholder echo in useVoiceLoop with real Claude conversation streaming. The user's speech is sent to `/api/chat`, text deltas stream into TTSClient for sentence-buffered playback, and conversation history accumulates across turns. On menu extraction completion, the app automatically speaks a menu overview before the user says anything (MENU-05). This plan delivers the complete voice-based menu exploration experience.
